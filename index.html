About

Hack'nTech is a student-run hackathon under the aegis of TecSetCo with the collaboration of various sponsors and communities. The goal is to provide a competitive platform for all tech enthusiasts.
It's a 36-hour long hackathon that strives to engage a wide range of talented developers and engineers for comprehensive learning, building, and collaborating It will provide a plethora of opportunities for tech-savvy individuals to flaunt their abilities, improvise using new and old tech trends, and have their talents recognized and appreciated. The creativity, originality, business value, impact, and long-term use will be judged by a panel of expert Judges.
